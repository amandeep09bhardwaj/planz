Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

PLANZ ("we", "our", or "the app") is a task planning and scheduling app developed by Amandeep Bhardwaj. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Tasks, routines, and availability settings — stored locally using Core Data. This data syncs across your devices via Apple iCloud if enabled.
  • Preferences — theme, notification settings, and onboarding state stored in UserDefaults.
  • Photos — if you attach images to tasks, they are stored locally on your device.

Data Stored on Our Servers (Firebase)

  • Anonymous identifier — we use Firebase Anonymous Authentication. No email, name, or password is collected.
  • Referral data — your referral code, redemption count, and reward status are stored in Google Cloud Firestore.
  • Shared Plans — if you create or join a shared plan, the plan name, member IDs, display names, and shared tasks are stored in Firestore.
  • Device token — if you enable push notifications, your FCM device token is stored to deliver notifications for shared plan activity.

Data Collected by Third Parties

  • Google AdMob — displays ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and interaction data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous usage data (app opens, feature usage, crash reports) to help us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports including device model, OS version, and stack traces to help us fix bugs.
  • Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT) — we request your permission before allowing ad-related tracking. You can decline without affecting app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide task scheduling, routines, and planning features
  • To sync your data across devices via iCloud
  • To enable shared plans and push notifications
  • To manage the referral reward system
  • To display relevant ads (free tier only)
  • To analyze app usage and fix crashes

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with:

  • Google (Firebase & AdMob) — for authentication, data storage, analytics, crash reporting, and advertising as described above.
  • Apple (iCloud & StoreKit) — for cloud sync and in-app purchase processing.
  • Shared Plan members — if you join a shared plan, your display name and tasks are visible to other plan members.

4. Data Retention

  • Local data is retained until you delete the app or clear it manually.
  • Firebase data (referral, shared plans) is retained as long as your anonymous account exists.
  • You can delete your shared plans from within the app at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

PLANZ is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication and does not require any personal information.

6. Your Rights

  • You can disable ad tracking by declining the ATT prompt or via Settings > Privacy > Tracking.
  • You can disable iCloud sync in your device's iCloud settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove all local data.
  • To request deletion of your Firebase data, contact us at the email below.
